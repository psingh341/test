Testimonial

"I began working with Sam around a year and a half ago at the age of 15 after following him and his brother @the_one_rep_max for quite some time. At the time I was around 170lbs and totaling 920lbs, obviously quite new to lifting. I’d searched for quite some time for a coach, DM’ing a handful of popular lifters I’d followed, yet I didn’t have much luck finding anyone who was nearly as personable, caring, and genuinely helpful as Sam. Now 17, I am totaling 1260lbs and weighing 210lbs, and I regard my choice to begin to work with Sam as one of the best decisions I’ve ever made.
